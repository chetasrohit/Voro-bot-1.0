## ðŸ‘¤ User Guide (Student)

The Student interface is designed for ease of use, allowing students to quickly find information about the college.

### 1. Login

- Visit the **User Login** page.
- Enter your **Full Name**, **Grade**, **Roll Number**, and **Email**.
- Click **"Start Chatting"**.
  - _Note: If it's your first time, an account is automatically created for you._

### 2. Using the Chatbot

Once logged in, you will see the main chat interface.

- **Main Menu**: Click on any topic button (e.g., "Admissions", "Fees") to explore that section.
- **Drill Down**: After selecting a topic, you will see a list of specific questions (Options). Click one to get the answer.
- **Ask Questions**: You can type questions directly into the input box (e.g., _"How do I pay fees?"_). The bot will try to find the best matching answer.
- **Navigation**: Use the **Back Arrow** (top-right) to return to the previous menu.
- **Dark Mode**: Click the **Moon Icon** in the header to switch between Light and Dark themes.

### 3. Sending Feedback

- Click the **Comment Icon** in the header.
- Type your message or suggestion and click **Send**.
- Admins will review your feedback.
